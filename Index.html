Welcome to Hakkore Technologies

Hakkore Technologies a renowned original equipment manufacture of reciprocation piston compressors was established in the year 1989 by Mr. G.Dhanraj. Our manufacturing unit is located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu having a turnover of 1 core per annum.

Focusing his mind on the scope, which is to provide his customers with the best products and ensuring customer satisfaction, the company with stood for 25 years in celebrating its silver jubilee in 2014. Initially the company started on a lower key concentrating only on local market but gradually gaining them in Chennai, Andhra Pradesh, Kerala, Karnataka, Mumbai, Kolkata and Gujarat touching more than 2000 and increasing customers and meeting their requirements along with prompt after sale service. In 2014 the company opened its regional marketing office in Ekkattuthangal, Chennai which will be solely handling the marketing and after sales service for Chennai customers. ...
